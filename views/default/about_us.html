Why We Were Founded

This website is a product of four computer science students coming together to solve a problem at Miramonte High School. The problem is the confusion and lack of information surrounding clubs at Miramonte. We wanted to create a database compiling info about the many awesome clubs that Miramonte students can join.

What began as a simple idea for an AP Computer Science project has now transformed into an exciting new endeavor with thrilling possibilities!

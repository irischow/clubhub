Sign Up for an Account

  1. Clicking on "Sign Up" in the "Log In" menu at the top right-hand corner of the page.
  2. Complete the form and click on the "Sign Up" button.
  3. You will be sent a verification email. Click on the link in the email to verify your email address. You will now be able to log in.

Join clubs

Go to the "Clubs" page and select the clubs you wish to join. Click on the "Join" button at the bottom of the page.

Club Presidents

  1. Email us to add you as the president of your club.
  2. Once you are marked as president, you can update your club's description, email, meeting time and place, and name. You can also upload a photo of your club!

Want to be featured as club of the month?

Please fill out this Google Form! The deadline to fill out the form is the 20th of the previous month.

Contact Us!

Email clubs.miramonte@gmail.com for all of your needs and we will respond as quickly as possible!

For Club Members

  1. Create an account using the Log In button in the top right-hand corner.
  2. Select the clubs you wish to join under the "Clubs" heading and click join.

For Club Presidents

  1. Make an account using the Log In button in the top right-hand corner.
  2. Within 1-2 days of you creating your account, we will mark you as the president for you club.
  3. Once you are marked as president, you can update your club's description, email, meeting time and place, and name. You can also upload a photo of your club!
